# Pool Service & Maintenance Operations

Run a pool service business with real numbers — routing, chemical pricing, equipment repair margins, licensing, and growth from solo tech to multi-crew operation.

## What This Covers

### Service Pricing & Packages
- **Weekly maintenance**: $125-200/month residential, $300-800/month commercial
- **Chemical-only service**: $80-120/month (lower margin, good entry point)
- **Green-to-clean**: $250-500 one-time (high margin, fast cash)
- **Opening/closing** (seasonal markets): $200-350 per service
- **Equipment repair**: $85-150/hour labor + 30-50% parts markup
- **Pool remodel/resurface**: $5,000-15,000+ (subcontract or in-house)
- **Target**: 60%+ gross margin on recurring maintenance, 70%+ on repairs

### Chemical Management
- **Chlorine (liquid)**: $3-5/gallon, use 1-2 gal/week per pool
- **Tabs (trichlor)**: $80-120/bucket (50lb), 1-3 tabs/week per pool
- **Muriatic acid**: $8-12/gallon, 0.5-1 gal/month per pool
- **Chemical cost per pool**: $30-60/month (keep under 25% of service price)
- **Buy wholesale**: Pool Corp (POOL), SCP Distributors, local co-ops
- **Water chemistry targets**: pH 7.2-7.6, chlorine 1-3 ppm, alkalinity 80-120 ppm, CYA 30-50 ppm
- **Salt pools**: check cell, maintain 2,700-3,400 ppm salt, clean cell quarterly

### Route Optimization
- **Solo operator target**: 15-20 pools/day, 5 days/week = 75-100 accounts
- **Time per pool**: 15-25 minutes (maintenance), 45-90 minutes (repair)
- **Drive time**: cluster routes by neighborhood, max 10 min between stops
- **Revenue per truck**: $9,000-16,000/month at 80 accounts × $125-200
- **Seasonal adjustment**: weekly in summer, biweekly in winter (warm climates stay weekly)
- **Route density**: 8+ pools within 3-mile radius = profitable cluster

### Equipment & Startup
- **Truck setup**: $3,000-5,000 (tank, chemical rack, hose reel, tool storage)
- **Test kit**: Taylor K-2006 ($80) or digital LaMotte ($300+)
- **Basic tools**: pole, net, brush, vacuum head, leaf canister — $500-800
- **Chemical inventory**: $500-1,000 starting stock
- **Total startup**: $8,000-15,000 (truck + tools + chemicals + insurance + licensing)
- **Used truck**: $15,000-25,000 for reliable work vehicle

### Licensing & Compliance
- **CPO (Certified Pool Operator)**: required in most states for commercial, $300-400 exam
- **State contractor license**: varies — FL requires CPC license, CA requires C-53, TX requires registration
- **EPA compliance**: chemical storage, disposal, SDS sheets on every truck
- **Insurance**: GL $500K-1M ($1,200-2,500/year), commercial auto ($1,500-3,000/year), workers comp when hiring
- **Health department**: commercial pools require weekly logs, some states require licensed operator of record

### Hiring & Crew Scaling
- **First hire**: at 80-100 accounts (you're maxed solo)
- **Tech pay**: $15-22/hour (entry), $20-30/hour (experienced/CPO certified)
- **Revenue per tech**: cover 60-80 pools/week = $8,000-14,000/month revenue
- **Target labor cost**: 30-35% of service revenue
- **Training**: 2-4 weeks ride-along, water chemistry test before solo routes
- **Retention**: pay CPO certification, performance bonuses on upsells, route ownership pride

### Growth Playbook
**Stage 1: Solo Operator (0-80 accounts, $0-$16K/month)**
- Door-to-door in target neighborhoods, Nextdoor posts, Google Business profile
- Focus on route density — say no to far-flung pools
- Master chemistry and speed, build 5-star Google reviews

**Stage 2: First Crew (80-200 accounts, $16K-$36K/month)**
- Hire first tech, hand off densest route
- Add repair services (pump, filter, heater) for 2-3x margins
- Start commercial accounts (HOAs, apartments, hotels)

**Stage 3: Multi-Crew (200-500 accounts, $36K-$90K/month)**
- 3-5 trucks on road, office/dispatch person
- Pool remodel partnerships or in-house resurface crew
- Equipment sales (Pentair, Hayward dealer)
- Recurring revenue from chemical delivery subscriptions

**Stage 4: Market Leader (500+ accounts, $90K+/month)**
- Acquire competitor routes ($1,000-1,500 per account is standard)
- Franchise or expand to adjacent markets
- Automated billing, route optimization software (Skimmer, ServiceTitan)
- Sell chemical delivery subscription boxes direct to DIY pool owners

### Key Metrics
- **Customer acquisition cost**: $50-150 (door-to-door is cheapest)
- **Lifetime value**: $1,500-2,400/year × 3-5 year retention = $4,500-12,000
- **Monthly churn**: target under 3% (seasonal markets higher)
- **Revenue per account**: track monthly, flag any under $100
- **Chemical cost ratio**: keep under 25% of service revenue
- **Callbacks**: track per tech, target under 5% (water quality complaints)
