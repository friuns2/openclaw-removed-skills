# Client Communication Drafter

Stop staring at that email for 30 minutes. Describe the situation, get a send-ready email in seconds.

## What It Does

Handles every client email a freelancer dreads writing:

- **Project updates** — structured, professional, shows momentum
- **Scope change pushback** — firm but collaborative, protects your boundaries
- **Payment reminders** — 3-stage escalation (gentle > firm > final) with UK late payment law
- **Feedback requests** — specific questions, deadlines, keeps the project moving
- **Difficult conversations** — deadline slips, disagreements, project termination
- **Project wrap-ups** — handover, testimonial requests, referral prompts
- **Cold outreach & follow-ups** — concise, specific, one clear CTA

## Quick Start

```
/client-comms-drafter invoice #47 is 10 days overdue, client is a startup, usually pays on time
```

```
/client-comms-drafter client wants 3 extra pages added to the website, not in scope, how do I push back
```

```
/client-comms-drafter project update, finished the wireframes, need feedback by Friday to stay on track
```

## Why It Exists

Freelancers lose hours each week to client emails. The hard ones — chasing payments, pushing back on scope creep, delivering bad news — get procrastinated on for days. This skill writes them in seconds, with the right tone, every time.

## Who It's For

- Freelancers who hate writing emails (all of them)
- Anyone chasing a late payment and not sure how firm to be
- Freelancers dealing with scope creep
- Solo operators without an account manager to handle comms
