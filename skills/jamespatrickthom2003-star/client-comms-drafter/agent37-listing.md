# Agent37 Listing — Client Communication Drafter

## Skill Name
Client Communication Drafter

## Tagline
Stop staring at that email. Describe the situation, get a send-ready client email in seconds — payment chasing, scope pushback, updates, and difficult conversations handled.

## Category
Business Operations

## Description

**The problem:** Freelancers spend hours each week on client emails. The hard ones — chasing overdue payments, pushing back on scope creep, delivering bad news — get procrastinated on for days. That delay costs money and damages relationships.

**What this skill does:** Describe the situation. Get a professional, send-ready email:

- **Payment reminders** with 3-stage escalation (gentle, firm, final) and UK late payment law references
- **Scope change pushback** that's firm but keeps the relationship intact
- **Project updates** that show momentum and prompt decisions
- **Feedback requests** with specific questions and deadlines
- **Difficult conversations** — deadline slips, disagreements, terminations
- **Project wrap-ups** with testimonial requests and referral prompts
- **5 tone modes:** professional-warm, formal, casual, firm, diplomatic

**Why freelancers pay for this:** The "I've been staring at this email for 30 minutes" problem is universal. This solves it in 10 seconds.

## Example Prompts
- "Invoice #47 is 10 days overdue, they usually pay on time"
- "Client wants 3 extra pages, not in scope, need to push back nicely"
- "Project update — wireframes done, need feedback by Friday"
- "I messed up the deadline by a week, how do I tell them"
- "Project's done, want to ask for a testimonial without being weird"
