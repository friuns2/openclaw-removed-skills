## Description

**The problem:** 2.9 million UK freelancers now face quarterly digital tax submissions under Making Tax Digital. Most don't know their actual tax liability until January, don't track VAT thresholds, and can't categorise expenses correctly for Self Assessment.

**What this skill does:** Give it your income and expenses. Get back a complete, accurate tax breakdown in seconds:

- Income Tax across all bands (including the brutal 60% marginal rate above £100k)
- Class 4 and Class 2 National Insurance
- VAT threshold alerts (£90,000 rolling turnover)
- MTD quarterly submission summaries with deadlines
- Payment on Account calculations
- HMRC-aligned expense categorisation

**Why it's better than a spreadsheet:** It handles the PA taper, NI thresholds, VAT proximity warnings, and MTD deadlines automatically. No formulas to maintain. No rates to update.

**Who it's for:** UK sole traders, freelancers, self-employed contractors, anyone doing their own Self Assessment.
