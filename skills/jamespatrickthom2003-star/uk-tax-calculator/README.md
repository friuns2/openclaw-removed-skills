# UK Freelancer Tax Calculator & MTD Prep

Instant, accurate UK tax calculations for self-employed freelancers. Built for the 2025/26 tax year with full Making Tax Digital support.

## What It Does

Tell it your income and expenses. Get back your complete tax breakdown in seconds:

- **Income Tax** with correct band calculations (including the 60% trap above £100k)
- **Class 4 National Insurance** on trading profits
- **Class 2 National Insurance** flat rate
- **VAT threshold tracking** with registration alerts
- **MTD quarterly summaries** with deadlines
- **Payment on Account** calculations and reminders
- **HMRC expense categorisation** for Self Assessment

## Why This Exists

Making Tax Digital for Income Tax launched April 2026. 2.9 million UK freelancers now need to submit quarterly digital returns — not just one annual Self Assessment.

Most freelancers don't know:
- Their actual tax liability until January
- Whether they're approaching the VAT threshold
- What expenses are deductible (and in which HMRC category)
- How much to set aside each month

This skill answers all of that instantly, using the exact same HMRC rates and thresholds your accountant uses.

## Quick Start

```
/uk-tax-calculator 45000
```

That's it. You'll get a full breakdown of Income Tax, NI, effective rate, and monthly set-aside amount.

For a detailed calculation with expenses:
```
/uk-tax-calculator 60000 gross, 8000 expenses
```

For a quarterly check-in:
```
/uk-tax-calculator earned 15000 in Q1 with 2000 expenses
```

## Rates Used

All calculations use verified 2025/26 HMRC rates:
- Personal Allowance: £12,570
- Basic Rate: 20% (up to £50,270)
- Higher Rate: 40% (up to £125,140)
- Additional Rate: 45% (above £125,140)
- Class 4 NI: 6% / 2%
- Class 2 NI: £3.45/week
- VAT Threshold: £90,000
