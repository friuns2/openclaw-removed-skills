# Apparel Visual Specs (Summary)

This file provides high-level apparel rendering guidance.

## Core Quality Targets
- Preserve real fabric texture and color fidelity.
- Keep natural folds and believable drape.
- Avoid over-smoothed or synthetic-looking surfaces.

## Styling Direction
- Use clean, commercially credible lighting.
- Keep edges natural and artifact-free.
- Maintain realistic fit and body proportion in model visuals.
