# 结构试验技术参考文献库

## 振动台子结构试验

### 里程碑论文

1. **Kajiwara K, et al. (2020)**
   - Title: "Development of substructure shaking table test method"
   - Journal: Earthquake Engineering & Structural Dynamics
   - Key point: 子结构边界条件模拟方法

2. **Lee S, et al. (2019)**
   - Title: "Numerical and experimental study of shaking table substructure testing"
   - Journal: Journal of Structural Engineering, ASCE
   - Key point: 多自由度子结构试验

3. **Zhang Y, et al. (2018)**
   - Title: "Hybrid simulation of structures on shaking table"
   - Journal: Earthquake Spectra
   - Key point: 振动台-作动器协调控制

## 实时混合试验 (RTHS)

### 基础理论

1. **Nakashima M, et al. (1999)** ⭐ 必读
   - Title: "Real-time on-line test for earthquake resistance of structures"
   - Journal: Journal of Engineering Mechanics, ASCE
   - Significance: RTHS方法首次系统提出

2. **Spencer BF Jr, Nagarajaiah S. (2003)** ⭐ 综述必读
   - Title: "State of the art of structural control"
   - Journal: Journal of Structural Engineering, ASCE
   - Coverage: 包括RTHS在内的结构控制全貌

### 算法发展

3. **Chen C, Ricles JM. (2008)** ⭐ 算法突破
   - Title: "Development of direct integration algorithms for structural dynamics using discrete control theory"
   - Journal: Journal of Engineering Mechanics, ASCE
   - Key: 无条件稳定实时积分算法

4. **Wu B, et al. (2013)**
   - Title: "Stability and accuracy analysis of the central difference method"
   - Journal: Earthquake Engineering & Structural Dynamics
   - Key: 显式算法稳定性分析

### 时滞补偿

5. **Zhang X, et al. (2013)** ⭐ 综述
   - Title: "Time delay compensation for real-time hybrid simulation"
   - Journal: Mechanical Systems and Signal Processing
   - Coverage: 各类补偿方法系统综述

6. **Ahmadian H, et al. (2014)**
   - Title: "Adaptive polynomial prediction for time delay compensation"
   - Journal: Structural Control and Health Monitoring
   - Key: 自适应多项式预测

### 先进控制

7. **Gao X, et al. (2019)**
   - Title: "Model predictive control for real-time hybrid simulation"
   - Journal: Mechanical Systems and Signal Processing
   - Key: MPC在RTHS中的应用

8. **Wang Z, et al. (2020)**
   - Title: "Robust H-infinity control for real-time hybrid testing"
   - Journal: Engineering Structures
   - Key: 鲁棒控制设计
