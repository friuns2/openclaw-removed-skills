## Example 8: Weather-Adapted Outfit Decision

**User Input**:
```
Temperature dropped to 10 degrees today, still raining, what to wear to go out?
```

**Skill Output**:
```markdown
👔 Rainy Cold Weather Outfit Decision: Warm and Waterproof

🧥 Outfit Plan:
• Base layer: Thermal underwear/heat-tech innerwear
• Mid layer: Sweater/fleece
• Outer layer: Windproof waterproof jacket/hardshell
• Bottoms: Fleece-lined pants/thick jeans
• Footwear: Waterproof boots/thick-soled shoes (avoid canvas)
• Accessories: Umbrella + scarf + hat

📋 Reasoning:
• 10°C + rain: Wet cold weather, windproof and waterproof is key
• Three-layer system: Base warmth + mid insulation + outer wind/water protection
• Shoes must be waterproof, avoid wet cold feet
• Scarf and hat reduce heat loss

⚠️ Special Reminders:
• Bring spare socks (in case shoes get wet)
• Choose jacket with hood for double protection
• Keep a plastic bag in bag (for wet umbrella)

💡 If it's raining heavily:
Consider taxi/subway to minimize outdoor time
```

## Usage Tips

### 1. Provide More Info for Better Recommendations

**Basic**:
```
What should I eat for lunch?
```

**Better**:
```
What should I eat for lunch? Budget 50, want something light, short on time
```

### 2. Use Quick Commands for Fast Launch

```
/decide food      # Quick launch food decision
/decide outfit    # Quick launch outfit decision
/decide activity  # Quick launch activity decision
/decide buy headphones  # Quick launch purchase
