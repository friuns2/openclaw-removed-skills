# Opening Design

## Opening Gate

Treat the opening as a mandatory approval gate before first-batch prose drafting.

The opening gate exists to stop three common failures:
- the book does not light immediately
- the book lights quickly but has no follow-through
- the book has a flashy premise but no long-line support

Without an approved opening package, do not enter the first prose batch.

## Required file

Create and review:
- `04A_开篇设计.md`

## Core time horizons

### 前三章
Solve:
- 点火
- 显规
- 留钩

The first three chapters must show why the reader should keep going now, not later.

### 前十章
Solve:
- 主角驱动力
- 阶段目标
- 第一轮关系压力
- 第一轮代价显形

If the first ten chapters still cannot explain why the protagonist must keep moving, the opening is unstable.

### 前二十章
Solve:
- 卷级主线是否站住
- 代价体系是否成立
- 主要悬念是否在持续加压
- 是否已经显出长线承重能力

If the first twenty chapters still feel like disconnected local tasks, the opening gate should fail.

## Approval checklist

The opening gate passes only if:
- the project has a clear first-chapter ignition point
- the first three chapters have distinct tasks, not one blurred “推进剧情”
- the first ten chapters prove the protagonist has durable motivation
- the first twenty chapters show a usable volume-level mainline and cost system
- the retained novelty axes from the Cliche Exhaustion Loop are still visible in the opening package
- the user explicitly approves `04A_开篇设计.md`

Opening validation is not a license to restart wide ideation.
Use it to confirm delivery of retained novelty axes, not to reopen broad lane exploration.

## Common failure patterns

- long background explanation before any real conflict
- the hook appears, but the book cannot explain what sustains chapter 8-20
- the protagonist reacts well but does not actively drive the book
- the opening only sells a gimmick and hides the real mainline
- every chapter ends loudly, but none of them deepen pressure
