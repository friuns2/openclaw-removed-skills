# Platform Profiles

## Goal

Treat platform choice as a structural decision, not a late marketing label.

## 起点模式

Prefer this mode when the project wants:
- stronger system depth
- heavier long-line bearing
- more setup tolerance if the payoff engine is visible
- stronger volume architecture

Writing implications:
- the opening still must grab, but can carry slightly denser setup
- the mid-book must keep escalating through systems, factions, or line convergence
- chapter endings should pull, but total-structure strength matters more than pure shoutiness

## 番茄模式

Prefer this mode when the project wants:
- very fast readability
- extremely strong opening hook density
- immediate scene comprehension
- faster emotional and payoff cadence

Writing implications:
- the core selling point must land faster
- exposition friction must stay low
- chapter-end hooks must be frequent and obvious
- complicated setup without quick pressure will stall

## 通用模式

Use this when:
- the user has not committed to one platform
- the project wants a balanced middle path
- the team needs a stable default while still preserving lane identity

Writing implications:
- keep the opening clear and strong
- do not overfit to either slow-burn complexity or pure fast-food hook spam
- preserve enough structure to continue scaling later
