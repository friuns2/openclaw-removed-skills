# New Year - 新年旅游指南

## Basic Information - 基本信息
- **Date - 日期**：January 1 (often Dec 31 - Jan 2)
- **Characteristics - 特点**：New Year celebrations, fireworks, winter travel
- **Weather - 天气**：Winter in Northern Hemisphere, summer in Southern Hemisphere

## Travel Recommendations - 旅游推荐

### 🎆 Recommended Options - 推荐方向
**1. Iconic New Year's Eve Celebrations - 标志性跨年活动**
- New York (Times Square Ball Drop) - 纽约（时代广场落球）
- Sydney (Harbor Bridge Fireworks) - 悉尼（海港大桥烟花）
- London (Big Ben + Thames Fireworks) - 伦敦（大本钟+泰晤士河烟花）
- Paris (Eiffel Tower Light Show) - 巴黎（埃菲尔铁塔灯光秀）
- Dubai (Burj Khalifa Fireworks) - 迪拜（哈利法塔烟花）

**2. Winter Festivals - 冬季节日**
- Iceland (New Year's Eve bonfires) - 冰岛（新年篝火）
- Scotland (Hogmanay celebrations) - 苏格兰（除夕庆典）
- Japan (New Year temple visits) - 日本（新年寺庙参拜）

**3. Warm Escape - 温暖度假**
- Southeast Asia (Thailand, Vietnam) - 东南亚（泰国、越南）
- Australia (Summer New Year) - 澳大利亚（夏季新年）
- New Zealand (First country to see New Year) - 新西兰（最早看到新年的国家）

## Practical Tips - 实用建议

### Booking Advice - 预订建议
- **Flights/Hotels - 机票/酒店**：Book 2-3 months in advance
- **New Year's Eve events - 跨年活动**：Many require tickets purchased months ahead
- **Restaurants - 餐厅**：Special New Year's Eve menus sell out fast

### Practical Considerations - 实用提醒
- **Crowds - 人潮**：Popular spots will be packed - arrive early
- **Prices - 价格**：Significant premium (50%-100%)
- **Transport - 交通**：Public transport runs all night in major cities, but expect crowds
