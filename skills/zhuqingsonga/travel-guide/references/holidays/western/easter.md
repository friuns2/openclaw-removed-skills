# Easter - 复活节旅游指南

## Basic Information - 基本信息
- **Date - 日期**：First Sunday after full moon on or after March 21 (March/April)
- **Characteristics - 特点**：Spring celebration, religious significance, Easter eggs, chocolate
- **Weather - 天气**：Spring in Northern Hemisphere, beautiful blossoms

## Travel Recommendations - 旅游推荐

### 🐰 Recommended Options - 推荐方向
**1. Religious & Cultural - 宗教与文化**
- Vatican City/Easter Mass - 梵蒂冈/复活节弥撒
- Jerusalem (Holy Week processions) - 耶路撒冷（圣周游行）
- Seville (elaborate Easter processions) - 塞维利亚（精致复活节游行）
- Antigua Guatemala (colorful processions) - 安提瓜危地马拉（多彩游行）

**2. Spring Blossoms - 春花烂漫**
- Netherlands (Tulip fields + Keukenhof) - 荷兰（郁金香田+库肯霍夫）
- Japan (Cherry blossom season) - 日本（樱花季）
- Washington DC (Cherry blossoms) - 华盛顿特区（樱花）
- England (Bluebells + spring gardens) - 英国（蓝铃花+春季花园）

**3. Family-Friendly - 家庭友好**
- Germany (Easter markets) - 德国（复活节市场）
- Austria (Egg decorating traditions) - 奥地利（彩蛋装饰传统）
- Sweden (Easter celebrations) - 瑞典（复活节庆典）

## Practical Tips - 实用建议

### Booking Advice - 预订建议
- **Popular destinations - 热门目的地**：Netherlands, Spain, Italy book up fast
- **Holy Week - 圣周**：Especially busy in Catholic countries
- **Hotels - 酒店**：Book 2-3 months in advance

### Practical Considerations - 实用提醒
- **Business closures - 商家关门**：Many shops closed Easter Sunday/Monday
- **Public transport - 公共交通**：Reduced service on Easter day
- **Restaurants - 餐厅**：Book ahead for Easter lunch/dinner
