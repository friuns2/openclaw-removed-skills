# Thanksgiving - 感恩节旅游指南

## Basic Information - 基本信息
- **Date - 日期**：4th Thursday of November (US), 2nd Monday of October (Canada)
- **Characteristics - 特点**：Family gathering, turkey dinner, Black Friday shopping
- **Weather - 天气**：Fall in North America

## Travel Recommendations - 旅游推荐

### 🦃 Recommended Options - 推荐方向
**1. Classic Thanksgiving - 经典感恩节**
- New York (Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade) - 纽约（梅西感恩节游行）
- Boston (Colonial Thanksgiving) - 波士顿（殖民时期感恩节）
- Plymouth (First Thanksgiving site) - 普利茅斯（第一个感恩节地点）

**2. Fall Foliage - 秋季赏叶**
- New England (Vermont, New Hampshire, Maine) - 新英格兰（佛蒙特、新罕布什尔、缅因）
- Great Smoky Mountains - 大烟山
- Colorado (Aspen gold) - 科罗拉多（阿斯彭金黄）

**3. City Break - 城市度假**
- New York (pre-Christmas atmosphere + Black Friday) - 纽约（圣诞氛围+黑色星期五）
- Chicago (Thanksgiving parade + shopping) - 芝加哥（感恩节游行+购物）
- San Francisco (mild weather + wine country nearby) - 旧金山（温和气候+附近酒乡）

## Practical Tips - 实用建议

### Booking Advice - 预订建议
- **Flights - 机票**：One of the busiest travel times in US - book 2-3 months early
- **Hotels - 酒店**：Book early, especially in parade cities
- **Restaurants - 餐厅**：Thanksgiving dinner requires booking weeks in advance

### Practical Considerations - 实用提醒
- **Many businesses closed - 很多商家关门**：Nov 24-25, plan accordingly
- **Black Friday - 黑色星期五**：Major shopping day Nov 25, stores open early (or Thanksgiving night!)
- **Rental cars - 租车**：Book early, prices surge 2x-3x
